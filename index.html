Instructor: Professor Elvin Beach

We're engineering aluminum alloys that don't just resist breaking but bend without failing. Our work combines precise composition control with strategic heat treatments to manipulate how atoms arrange themselves. This mastery of atomic architecture transforms aircraft components and everyday objects, making them lighter, stronger, and more durable.
